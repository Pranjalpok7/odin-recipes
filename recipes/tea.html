Tea

Description

Chai tea, also known as masala chai, is a delicious and aromatic spiced tea that originated in India. It's made with a blend of black tea, aromatic spices, milk, and sweetener. Here's a simple recipe to make a classic cup of chai tea

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup whole milk (you can use any milk of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons black tea leaves or 4 black tea bags
  • 4-5 green cardamom pods
  • 4-5 whole cloves
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, sliced (optional)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1-2 tablespoons of sweetener (sugar, honey, or any other sweetener of your choice)
  • A pinch of ground black pepper (optional)
  • A pinch of ground nutmeg (optional)
  1. In a saucepan, add water and all the spices (cardamom pods, cloves, ginger, and cinnamon stick). Bring it to a boil and let it simmer for about 5 minutes. This will infuse the water with the flavors of the spices.
  2. Add the black tea leaves or tea bags to the spiced water. If you're using loose tea leaves, you can use a tea strainer or a muslin cloth to contain them.
  3. Let the tea steep in the spiced water for about 3-5 minutes or until it reaches your desired strength. The longer you steep, the stronger the tea flavor.
  4. Add the milk to the tea. You can adjust the amount of milk according to your preference, whether you like a stronger tea taste or a creamier texture.
  5. Add the milk to the tea. You can adjust the amount of milk according to your preference, whether you like a stronger tea taste or a creamier texture.
  6. Optional: For an extra kick, you can add a pinch of ground black pepper and ground nutmeg. These spices add complexity to the flavor, but they are optional.
  7. Once everything is well combined, bring the chai tea to a gentle simmer. Do not let it boil vigorously.
  8. Turn off the heat and strain the chai tea into your cups or mugs.
  9. Enjoy your homemade chai tea hot and savor the delightful blend of spices and flavors!

Note: Chai tea can be customized to suit your taste preferences. You can experiment with different spices, adjust the sweetness, or use different types of milk to create your perfect cup of chai.
